Core Capabilities

What Our AI Analyzes

A complete deep learning pipeline that transforms raw football footage into rich analytics

🎯

Player Detection

YOLO v5 detects and identifies every player, referee, and the ball in each frame with high accuracy.

📡

Object Tracking

ByteTrack assigns persistent IDs to each detected object, maintaining identity across all frames.

👕

Team Assignment

K-Means clustering segments jersey colors to automatically assign each player to their respective team.

⚽

Ball Possession

Tracks which player and team controls the ball, computing real-time possession percentage statistics.

📷

Camera Estimation

Optical flow estimates camera movement between frames to accurately measure player displacement on the field.

🏃

Speed & Distance

Perspective transformation converts pixel coordinates to real-world meters for accurate speed and distance.
